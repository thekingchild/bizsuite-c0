Questions SMEs ask before moving to BizSuite

Answers to the most common questions from the current BizSuite website, adapted for this page.

What is BizSuite?

BizSuite is a cloud business management system that integrates purchase and sales management, finance, CRM, POS, expenses, service staff, and reporting into a single platform.

Do I have to pay for updates to the latest version?

No. Updates are available to everyone. Because BizSuite is a SaaS solution, subscribers receive the same updates at the same time.

What hardware does it support?

BizSuite supports internet-enabled devices with modern browsers, barcode scanners, thermal printers with ESC/POS commands, and label printers.

Does it support tax management?

Yes. Taxes are customizable, including VAT, GST, and grouped tax structures. You can apply them while creating purchases and sales, then review the related tax reports for a period.

Is BizSuite available in my country?

Yes. BizSuite is accessible in most countries.

How safe is BizSuite?

BizSuite uses HTTPS, HSTS, secure password handling, and routine account backups stored on a remote dedicated server.

What payment methods do you accept?

Online payment via Flutterwave and offline bank transfer are accepted.

I have a technical problem, who do I email?

Contact hello@ohrsystems.ng or chat directly with a representative on WhatsApp.

Do you need assistance? Let’s discuss

Setting up may require expert guidance. BizSuite consultants can help when you are starting a new business, scaling an existing operation, or migrating from another solution.
